This is the first iteration of the London OpeRations Day (LORD), which brings together researchers in Operations and associated fields from the London area, broadly defined. LORD is a single-track workshop consisting of a limited number (7-8) of invited presentations and ample free time for interaction and discussion among participants. The workshop will also feature a poster session to give an opportunity to PhD students/postdocs to present their work. We welcome talks on any subject of interest to the community.

You can use this template by cloning all the files from this GitHub repo here. This template was created from a website I made for MathConnections 2019, in case you want to see an example of this template being used in the wild.
