This is the first iteration of the London Operations Researchers Day (LORD), which brings together researchers in operations and associated fields from the London area, broadly defined. LORD is a single-track workshop consisting of a limited number of invited presentations and ample free time for interaction and discussion among participants. The workshop will be held at London Business School on Friday, April 19, 2024.

The workshop will also feature a poster session to give an opportunity to PhD students/postdocs to present their work. Those wishing to apply to apply for the poster session will need to fill out a form (to be created). Acceptance notifications will subsequently be sent out along with instructions.

Please stay tuned for more news about LORD!
